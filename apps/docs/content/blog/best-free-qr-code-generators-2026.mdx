**What makes QRdx genuinely free:**

## 2. QRKIT — Best for Simple Static QR Codes

**useqrkit.com** offers unlimited static QR codes with no signup and 15+ QR types. It's fast and no-nonsense if you just need a standard QR code with basic customization.

**What's good:**
- Unlimited static QR codes, no account
- 5 free dynamic codes with account creation
- Supports URL, WiFi, vCard, email, phone, and more

**What's missing:** No scan analytics on the free tier, no developer API, no open-source code.

---

## 3. QR Code Monkey — Best for Static High-Quality QR Codes

**qrcode-monkey.com** has been around since 2012 and generates high-resolution static QR codes with good customization options.

**What's good:**
- Unlimited free QR codes (static only)
- Logo support
- Multiple dot patterns
- High-resolution downloads

**What's missing:** Dynamic QR codes require a paid plan. Zero analytics. No developer API. Closed-source SaaS.

According to tests, QR Code Monkey's static QR codes scan reliably at 150+ DPI — suitable for print at most sizes.

---

## 4. QR-Verse — Best SaaS Alternative for Non-Developers

**qr-verse.com** is a newer entrant offering unlimited dynamic QR codes with full analytics. It requires an account but has no hard limits on the free tier.

**What's good:**
- Unlimited dynamic QR codes
- Full scan analytics (location, device, count)
- 20+ QR types
- AI art QR code generation

**What's missing:** Requires account creation (no truly anonymous generation). No open-source code. No developer npm package or API.

## 5. QR Tiger — Avoid the Free Tier

**qrcode-tiger.com** is one of the most marketed QR code generators, but the free tier is barely usable:

- Only 3 QR codes on the free tier
- 500 scans per code (then the QR stops working unless you upgrade)
- Ads shown on the QR page redirect

For anyone comparing QR Tiger free vs. QRdx free: QRdx wins on every dimension.

## 6. Flowcode — Very Limited Free

**flowcode.com** limits the free tier to 2 QR codes with 500 total scans before requiring a paid upgrade. It's better suited to enterprise marketing teams than individual users.

## 7. Canva QR Code Generator

Canva generates basic static QR codes within its design tool. It requires an account and the QR feature only produces standard square codes with no pattern customization or analytics. Useful if you're already designing in Canva and just need a plain QR code, but not a standalone QR code tool.
