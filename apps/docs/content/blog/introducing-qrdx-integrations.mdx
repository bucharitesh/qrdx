Today, we're excited to announce a major new addition to QRdx: **Integrations**.

QRdx has always been about giving you the most powerful, most beautiful QR code experience possible. But your workflow doesn't live in isolation – you use other tools, other platforms, other services. Starting today, QRdx connects to them.

## What are QRdx Integrations?

QRdx Integrations let you connect third-party tools directly to your QRdx account. Once connected, these integrations unlock new capabilities inside the QRdx editor and dashboard that simply weren't possible before.

All integrations are authorized via **OAuth 2.0**, meaning you never share a password with us. You grant access, we store tokens securely with end-to-end encryption, and your credentials are automatically refreshed in the background so things just keep working.

You can find, connect, and manage all integrations from **Settings → Integrations**.

## Flagship Integrations

We're shipping two integrations today:

### Dub.sh — Short links with advanced analytics

[Dub.sh](https://dub.co) is the open-source link management platform. With the Dub integration, you can generate short links for your QR codes directly inside QRdx — without ever leaving the editor.

Once connected, you can:

- **Create short links** – Turn any long URL into a clean, branded short link
- **Track link analytics** – See click counts, referrers, devices, and locations
- **Use custom domains** – Connect your own domain via your Dub workspace
- **Set link expiration** – Expire links on a date you choose
- **Generate QR codes** – Round-trip: create a short link and QR code together

The integration uses Dub's OAuth 2.0 flow with PKCE for added security. You authorize QRdx to access your Dub workspaces and links — and we only request the exact scopes we need: `workspaces.read`, `workspaces.write`, `links.read`, and `links.write`.

### Google Drive — Import assets from your Drive

Creating a QR code with a custom logo or brand image? With the Google Drive integration, you can browse and import images directly from your Drive — no downloading and re-uploading required.

Once connected, you can:

- **Browse your Drive files** – See all your images in a file picker inside QRdx
- **Import images for QR codes** – Use any Drive image as a logo or background
- **Search for files** – Find the right asset fast with real-time search
- **Preview thumbnails** – See what you're picking before you import it

## How OAuth works in QRdx

Here's the flow when you connect an integration:

1. Click **Connect** on an integration in your settings.
2. You're redirected to the provider's OAuth authorization page (Dub, Google, etc.).
3. You grant QRdx the permissions it requests — scoped to exactly what's needed.
4. You're redirected back to QRdx and the connection is established.

Tokens are stored **encrypted at rest** and never exposed to the client. When a token is close to expiring, QRdx automatically refreshes it in the background — you never have to reconnect.

## Build your own (soon)

We've architected the integrations platform to be fully extensible. Under the hood, each integration is a self-contained module with a config, a typed API client, and a UI layer. Adding a new provider is a matter of implementing a standard interface.

We're planning to open up integrations to community contributions — so if there's a tool you'd love to see connected to QRdx, stay tuned. Or open an issue on [GitHub](https://github.com/bucharitesh/qrdx) and let us know.
