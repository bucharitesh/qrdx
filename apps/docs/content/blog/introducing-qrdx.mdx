Introducing QRdx – an open-source, AI-powered QR code generator that lets you create beautiful, fully customizable QR codes for your brand, business, or project.

## AI-Powered QR Generation

QRdx leverages AI to help you generate stunning QR code themes in seconds. Describe the style you want – whether it's minimal, bold, retro, or futuristic – and our AI will craft a unique theme tailored to your vision.

On top of intelligent theme generation, QRdx provides a powerful playground with real-time preview, giving you instant visual feedback as you refine your designs.

## Full Customization

Every element of your QR code is customizable:

- **Colors & Gradients** – Pick solid colors or create smooth gradients for the body, eyes, and background.
- **Patterns** – Choose from a variety of body and eye patterns to give your QR code a distinctive look.
- **Logos** – Embed your brand logo directly in the center of the QR code.
- **Frames & Labels** – Add call-to-action frames with custom text.
- **Export Formats** – Download your QR codes in PNG, JPG, or SVG at any resolution.

## Fully Open Source

QRdx is fully open source, released under the MIT license. You can clone, modify, and deploy your own instance, or integrate the `qrdx` React component directly into your app.

Here's a link to the GitHub repository: [github.com/bucharitesh/qrdx](https://github.com/bucharitesh/qrdx)

Whether you're a designer crafting branded experiences, a business tracking engagement, or a developer building a QR platform – QRdx gives you the tools to create QR codes that are as beautiful as they are functional.
